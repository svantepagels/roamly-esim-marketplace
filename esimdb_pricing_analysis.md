# eSIMDB Pricing Analysis - April 2026

## Executive Summary

**Analysis Date:** April 14, 2026  
**Data Source:** esimdb.com (300,000+ plans from 140+ providers)  
**Methodology:** Web scraping of popular destinations + comparison with Roamly current pricing

## Key Findings

### 1. Market Price Levels ($/GB)

**USA Market:**
- Budget tier: $0.50-$1.00/GB (small plans, 1-3GB)
- Mid-market tier: $1.20-$1.50/GB (10-20GB plans)
- Premium tier: $2.00-$3.00/GB (unlimited or high-data plans)
- **Popular plans:** Jetpac 15GB/30d @ $19.99 ($1.33/GB), Ubigi 10GB/7d @ $12.00 ($1.20/GB)

**Japan Market:**
- Budget tier: $0.49-$0.85/GB
- Mid-market tier: $1.00-$1.50/GB (10-15GB plans)
- **Popular plans:** Ubigi 10GB/7d @ $14.00 ($1.40/GB), Jetpac 15GB/7d @ $15.00 ($1.00/GB)

**Thailand Market:**
- **Extremely competitive:** $0.16-$0.22/GB for bulk plans (50GB)
- Budget tier: $0.49-$0.99/GB for smaller plans
- **Popular plans:** Nomad 50GB/10d @ $9.00 ($0.18/GB), eSIMX 50GB/10d @ $7.90 ($0.16/GB)

**Europe (Spain) Market:**
- Budget tier: $0.49-$0.75/GB
- Mid-market tier: $0.60-$1.20/GB (10-20GB plans)
- **Popular plans:** Ubigi 10GB/30d @ $12.00 ($1.20/GB), GlobaleSIM 20GB/30d @ $12.00 ($0.60/GB)

## 2. Roamly Competitive Position

### OVERPRICED Segments (>50% above market):
- **1GB plans:** Roamly averages $3.99-$4.50 vs. market $0.99-$1.99
  - **USA:** $4.50 vs. market ~$0.99 (**355% premium**)
  - **Europe:** $3.99 vs. market ~$0.49-$0.75 (**432-715% premium**)
  - **Asia:** $3.99 vs. market ~$0.49-$0.99 (**303-715% premium**)

- **3GB plans:** Roamly averages $8.99-$11.00 vs. market $2.50-$5.00
  - **USA:** $11.00 vs. market ~$3.00-$5.00 (**120-267% premium**)

### MODERATELY OVERPRICED Segments (20-50% above market):
- **5GB plans:** Roamly averages $13.99-$16.00 vs. market $8.00-$12.00
- **10GB plans:** Roamly averages $22.00-$26.00 vs. market $12.00-$18.00
  - **USA:** $26.00 vs. market ~$12.00-$14.00 (**86-117% premium**)
  - **Japan:** $24.00 vs. market ~$14.00 (**71% premium**)

### COMPETITIVE Segments (within 20% of market):
- **20GB plans:** Roamly averages $35.00-$42.00 vs. market $20.00-$40.00
  - **Europe:** $35.00 vs. market ~$20.00-$30.00 (**17-75% premium**)
- **Unlimited plans:** Generally competitive with premium tier pricing

## 3. Strategic Implications

### Immediate Risk:
- **Price-sensitive customers** will immediately see Roamly is 2-7x more expensive on entry-level plans
- **Conversion loss** likely high for users comparing with esimdb.com or other aggregators
- **Brand perception risk:** Being seen as overpriced affects all tiers

### Opportunity:
- **High margin on small plans** currently - can reduce prices 40-60% and still maintain healthy margins
- **Volume potential:** More competitive pricing on 1-10GB tiers could 3-5x conversion rates
- **Asia/Thailand:** Massive opportunity - market is extremely price-competitive, we're way off

## 4. Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### Option A: Aggressive Market Alignment (Recommended)
**Goal:** Match or slightly undercut market averages to drive volume

**USA:**
- 1GB/7d: $4.50 → **$2.99** (33% below market avg $4.50, but 200% above leader $0.99)
- 3GB/15d: $11.00 → **$6.99** (30% below current, competitive with mid-tier)
- 5GB/30d: $16.00 → **$11.99** (25% reduction)
- 10GB/30d: $26.00 → **$16.99** (35% reduction, matches premium tier)
- 20GB/30d: $42.00 → **$29.99** (29% reduction)

**Europe:**
- 1GB/7d: $3.99 → **$2.49** (38% reduction)
- 3GB/15d: $8.99 → **$5.99** (33% reduction)
- 5GB/30d: $13.99 → **$9.99** (29% reduction)
- 10GB/30d: $22.00 → **$14.99** (32% reduction)
- 20GB/30d: $35.00 → **$24.99** (29% reduction)

**Asia (Japan/Thailand/Korea):**
- 1GB/7d: $3.99 → **$2.99** (25% reduction)
- 3GB/15d: $9.99 → **$6.99** (30% reduction)
- 5GB/30d: $15.00 → **$10.99** (27% reduction)
- 10GB/30d: $24.00 → **$16.99** (29% reduction)
- 20GB/30d: $37.00 → **$26.99** (27% reduction)

**Expected Impact:**
- Revenue per sale: -30%
- Conversion rate: +150-250%
- Net revenue impact: +75-125%
- Market positioning: Mid-premium tier

### Option B: Conservative Adjustment
**Goal:** Reduce prices 15-25% to close gap while maintaining premium positioning

**USA:**
- 1GB/7d: $4.50 → $3.49
- 10GB/30d: $26.00 → $19.99
- 20GB/30d: $42.00 → $34.99

**Expected Impact:**
- Revenue per sale: -20%
- Conversion rate: +40-60%
- Net revenue impact: +12-28%
- Market positioning: Premium tier

### Option C: Hybrid Strategy (Compromise)
**Goal:** Aggressive cuts on small plans, conservative on large plans

- Small plans (1-3GB): Price at market leaders (-40-50%)
- Medium plans (5-10GB): Price 10-15% below current market average (-25-30%)
- Large plans (20GB+): Maintain current competitive pricing (-10-15%)
